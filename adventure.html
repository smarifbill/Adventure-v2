An Adventure In The Woods

by Steffny.Marif Bill

It was a bright sunny Saturday morning. I usually ride my bicycle down to the lake to go for a swim but this time I decided to take a walk in the woods. As I walked, I listened to the sound of the birds chirping and watched the trees sway as the wind grew stronger. Suddenly, I heard a howl, I turned around, to my suprise there was a fox standing nearby. Shivering with fear, I didn't know what to do. I only thought of two options.
